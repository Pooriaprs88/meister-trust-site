What We Offer

At Meister Trust Services (MTS), we combine decades of industry expertise with innovative technology to deliver safe, reliable, and efficient solutions.
We support your projects to meet the highest standards for safety, quality, and productivity.

1. Construction & Facility Safety Inspections and Audits

Build a safer, stronger job site with expertise that goes beyond checklists. At MTS, our experienced inspectors bring real tradesman insight to every project — identifying risks before they become a problem.

From detailed audits to on-site support, we help your team maintain a proactive safety culture that drives performance and trust.

  • Pre-job safety programs, hazard identification, and risk control planning
  • Led by construction professional and NCSO with over 30 years of combined hands-on experience
  • Field inspections conducted with a tradesman's eye — ensuring your safety program is truly working where it matters most

2. Commissioning & Start-Up

At MTS, we ensure your mechanical systems are built precisely to engineered drawings and specifications, supporting you in every phase — from preservation and testing to commissioning and start-up.

With decades of construction and inspection experience, we bring clarity and confidence to every stage of your project.

3. System Monitoring & Condition Assessment

At MTS, we perform comprehensive field walk-downs to verify system integrity and identify potential issues before they impact performance. From insulation and corrosion checks to valve and support verification, our assessments ensure every component functions as intended — even under demanding conditions.

We also evaluate thermal expansion, contraction, and winterization requirements to help your systems perform year-round with minimal risk.

4. Analysis & Performance Testing

Optimize safety, efficiency, and compliance through precise system testing and monitoring. At MTS, we conduct detailed analysis to ensure compliance and advanced performance.

Using analyzers to ensure environmental emissions are within limits.

Flow and pressure verification. Preventative maintenance support, professionally planned and done based on decades of maintenance experience.

5. Drone Solutions

See your projects from a new perspective — safely, efficiently, and in real time. At MTS, our professional drone pilots work alongside certified inspectors to deliver detailed, high-resolution imaging and data-driven insights that support faster, safer, and more accurate decision-making.

Whether you need visual inspections, progress monitoring, or advanced surveying, our drone services provide cost-effective, non-intrusive solutions that reduce risk and eliminate the need for costly scaffolding or aerial work platforms.

Who We Are

Pride • Passion • Workmanship

  • Why "Meister"?

    The Foundation of Excellence

    Meister is the German word for Master—the highest level of education as a craftsman. It represents pride, passion, and workmanship. This is my understanding of being a professional in the industry.

  • Why "Trust"?

    The Base of Business

    Trust is the most important foundation in our industry. It's the base of business relationships and long-term success. Without trust, there can be no meaningful partnership or results.

  • Why "Service"?

    Results for Your Business

    Service is the base of success. With 40 years of experience, I deliver results that matter. From construction to commissioning, safety to maintenance—all focused on your project success.

Alexander Gross

Expert & Founder

I started my career in the pipe trades in 1984. Journeyman exam in 1987, Master college and exams in 1992. Self-employed with a service company in 1993, specializing in boiler system services.

Moved to Canada in 2008. Red Seal Plumber in 2009, Red Seal Steam and Pipefitter in 2018, NCSO in 2014. Since 2012, I've been dedicated to major industrial projects.

My expertise lies in system completion, commissioning, and start-up within the oil and gas industry. I focus on construction safety, quality, and documentation — no shortcuts, no punches. Done means done.

Safety Record: Never a Lost Time Injury (LTI) since 1984 for myself or my crew members.

Learning never ends. Always room for improvement. All about teamwork and pride.
